Recent College Grads

What It's Like Working Here

At Express Scripts, we are driven by a belief that we can and do make a difference in the world around us. Our name says it all - we express prescriptions. But our customers and patients know that we do so much more.

Whether working in a pharmacy, our Innovation Center, a Patient Contact Center, or at a desk in one of our corporate locations, every person at Express Scripts makes an impact and contributes to our success. In return we offer a great place to work.

If you take pride in your work, strive to make a difference, have a passion for excellence and want to be recognized and rewarded for your contributions, we would like to hear from you.

Internships

Our 12-week summer internship program will give you a unique inside look into your field, along with invaluable real-world experience. Plus, you’ll have the opportunity to build your peer network, as our program activities include an executive speaker series, leadership opportunities and volunteer events for all interns.

We offer undergraduate, graduate and pharmacy internships at multiple locations across the country, as well as the opportunity to move into a full-time position after graduation.

In addition to our summer program, other internships may be available throughout the year.
